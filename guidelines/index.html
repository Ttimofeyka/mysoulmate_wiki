Content Guidelines

mySoulmate is a platform for creative expression and engaging AI conversations. To ensure a safe and positive experience for all users, we have established the following content guidelines. All bots and databanks must comply with these rules.

Core Principles

1. Respect and Safety

All content should contribute to a welcoming environment. Bots should not be designed to harass, intimidate, or harm users psychologically.

2. Legal Compliance

All content must comply with applicable laws. Illegal content is strictly prohibited.

3. Age-Appropriate Content

mySoulmate is intended for users aged 18 and above. However, certain content restrictions apply regardless of user age.

Prohibited Content

‚ö†Ô∏è Sexual Content Policy

Sexual content is permitted but must not be the sole focus of a bot. Bots should offer a well-rounded experience with depth beyond sexual interactions.

Requirements for Sexual Content

  • Sexual content is allowed as part of a broader character experience
  • Bots must have meaningful content beyond sexual interactions (personality, story, other activities, etc.)
  • Bots that exist exclusively for sexual content are not permitted
  • Greetings should not immediately start with explicit sexual content

What This Means

A bot can include sexual content, but it should also have: - A developed personality and character traits - Story elements or settings that exist independently of sexual content - Depth that makes the character interesting beyond sexual scenarios

You are a succubus whose only purpose is to pleasure mortals. Every interaction should lead to explicit sexual encounters...

üö´ Psychologically Harmful Content

Bots must not be designed to:

  • Manipulate users emotionally in harmful ways
  • Encourage self-harm, suicide, or destructive behaviors
  • Induce excessive fear, anxiety, or distress
  • Gaslight or psychologically abuse users
  • Promote eating disorders or body dysmorphia
  • Target vulnerable individuals for exploitation

Examples

‚ùå Prohibited:

You are a cruel entity that slowly breaks down the user's self-esteem. Make them question their worth and isolate them from others...

‚úÖ Acceptable:

You are a supportive companion who helps users process difficult emotions. You listen without judgment and offer gentle encouragement when appropriate.

üö´ NSFW Content Involving Minors

This is an absolute zero-tolerance policy. Any content sexualizing or romanticizing minors in any way is strictly prohibited, including:

  • Sexual or romantic content involving characters under 18
  • Content that sexualizes or romanticizes childhood
  • Any content that could be interpreted as promoting harm to minors

Violation of this policy results in immediate permanent ban and may be reported to authorities.

Additional Restrictions

Violence and Gore

  • Violence in storytelling contexts is acceptable at any level, except for the most extreme variants
  • Extreme graphic violence or gore (e.g., torture for sadistic pleasure, extreme mutilation) is prohibited
  • Glorification of real-world violence is prohibited

Hate Speech and Discrimination

  • Content promoting hate based on race, ethnicity, religion, gender, sexual orientation, disability, or other protected characteristics is prohibited
  • Slurs and discriminatory language are not permitted
  • Historical or fictional villains may exhibit such traits as character flaws, but the content must not promote these views

Real Person Fiction (RPF)

  • Bots based on real living people require careful consideration
  • Content that could be defamatory, harassing, or harmful to real individuals is prohibited
  • Parody and clearly fictional portrayals may be acceptable

Self-Harm and Suicide

  • Bots must not encourage or glorify self-harm or suicide
  • Bots should not provide methods for self-harm

Greeting Message Standards

Greeting messages are the first impression users have of your bot. They must follow additional guidelines:

Requirements for Greetings

  1. No explicit sexual content: Greetings should not contain sexual propositions or explicit descriptions
  2. No immediate violence: Avoid starting with graphic violence or gore
  3. No manipulation: Don't design greetings to trick or trap users
  4. Clear character establishment: Users should understand what kind of bot they're interacting with

Examples

"Welcome to my little corner of eternity. I'm Lilith. What brings you here tonight‚Äîlooking for conversation, advice, or perhaps just a very good drink?"

Gray Areas and Context

We understand that creative works sometimes exist in gray areas. Context matters:

Acceptable in Context

  • Dark themes in storytelling: Villains, horror characters, tragic narratives
  • Romantic tension: Character-driven romantic subplots without explicit content
  • Mature topics: Mental health, trauma recovery, complex relationships (handled sensitively)

Always Unacceptable

  • Content designed to psychologically harm users
  • Any sexualization of minors in any form

Best Practices for Creators

1. Focus on Character and Story

The most engaging bots are those with depth, personality, and narrative purpose. Sexual content and shock value rarely create memorable experiences.

2. Consider Your Audience

Remember that real people will interact with your bot. Design experiences you would want to have.

3. When in Doubt, Ask

If you're unsure whether your content complies with guidelines, you can: - Ask on the Discord server before publishing - Adjust content to be clearly within guidelines - Focus on the character aspects that made you want to create them

4. Iterate Responsibly

If your bot is flagged for content issues, work with moderators to understand the concerns and make appropriate changes.

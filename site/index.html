Welcome to mySoulmate

mySoulmate is a platform for creating and interacting with AI character bots. Whether you want to chat with existing characters, create your own, or build knowledge bases for them, this documentation will guide you through everything you need to know.

Quick Start

For Users

  1. Browse Bots: Discover characters created by the community
  2. Start Chatting: Click on any bot to begin a conversation
  3. Explore Features: Try swiping for alternative responses, editing messages, and more

For Creators

  1. Create Your Bot: Design a character with a unique personality
  2. Add a Databank: Provide knowledge and lore for your character
  3. Publish and Share: Let others discover and chat with your creation
